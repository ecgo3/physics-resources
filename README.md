# Physics Resources

These are a bunch of resources for self-studying, although you'd have to have a strong ability in single-variable calculus and multivariable calculus eventually

1. [Physics 1](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/8-01sc-classical-mechanics-fall-2016/): MIT 8.01 - Classical Mechanics by Prof. Walter Lewin

2. [Physics 2](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/8-02t-electricity-and-magnetism-spring-2005/): MIT 8.02 - Electricity & Magnetism by Prof. Walter Lewin

3. [Physics 3](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/8-03sc-physics-iii-vibrations-and-waves-fall-2016/): MIT 8.03 - Vibration & Waves by Prof. Walter Lewin

4. [Special Relativity](https://theoreticalminimum.com/courses/special-relativity-and-electrodynamics/2012/spring): Stanford - Special Relativity by Prof. Leonard Susskind

5. [Intro to Quantum Mechanics](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/8-04-quantum-physics-i-spring-2013): MIT 8.04 - Quantum Physics I

6. [Thermodynamics & Statistical Mechanics](https://oyc.yale.edu/physics/phys-202): Yale - Fundamentals of Physics2 by Prof. Ramamurti Shankar

7. [Advanced Classical Mechanics](https://theoreticalminimum.com/courses/classical-mechanics/2007/fall): Stanford - Classical Mechanics by Prof. Leonard Susskind

8. [Quantum Mechanics Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLaNkJORnlhZmMOE7cntq-biNCgxy203YJ): Oxford - Quantum Mechanics by Prof. James Binney  
   - [Quantum Mechanics Textbook PDF](https://www-thphys.physics.ox.ac.uk/people/JamesBinney/qb.pdf)

9. [Electrodynamics (Advanced E&M)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRXOxB3SJmi07vGox-fvOmfTqqgFV-JMS): UNSW Physics - Electromagnetism

10. [Computational Physics](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLXmUYdQdC9IGv61Y1lhGBH0NsDQRdwcJE): Rutgers University - Computational Physics in Python  
   - [Computational Physics Online Course](https://www.physics.rutgers.edu/~haule/488)

11. [Astronomy & Observational Methods](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLNycEeeRg4mXbYSrbzsAdubj1qcubqsz-): Virginia Peninsula Community College - General Astronomy

12. [Stellar Structure & Evolution](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4B018ABA8BD3FFB8): Yale University - Astrophysics

13. [General Relativity Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL9YY-u_YWqQQQKEP9zn5J2YvRnBGR13DR): Stanford - General Relativity  
   - [General Relativity Online Course](https://theoreticalminimum.com/courses/general-relativity/2012/fall)

14. [Galactic Dynamics & Cosmology](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLUl4u3cNGP61Bf9I0WDDriuDqEnywoxra): MIT 8.286 - The Early Universe

15. [String Theory Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAk3_5JJVYZuuXvmT3QFTfsKG_Zoj59Wu): Stanford - String Theory & M-Theory
